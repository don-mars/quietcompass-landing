Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

What We Collect

When you sign up for a free worksheet or purchase a product, we collect your email address. That's it. We don't collect names, phone numbers, payment details (those are handled by Gumroad or Etsy), or any other personal information.

How We Use Your Email

We use your email to deliver the free resources you requested and to occasionally send you new tools, worksheets, and mental health resources. We will never sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties.

Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate:

ConvertKit (Kit) — email delivery and list management. Their privacy policy: kit.com/privacy

Gumroad — product hosting and payment processing. Their privacy policy: gumroad.com/privacy

Google Sheets — email backup storage via Google Apps Script.

GitHub Pages — website hosting. No tracking or cookies.

Cookies

Our landing page does not use cookies or tracking pixels. Third-party services (ConvertKit, Gumroad) may set their own cookies when you interact with their platforms.

Unsubscribe

Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link. Click it and you're removed immediately. You can also email us at quietcompass.store@gmail.com to request removal.

Data Deletion

If you want your email deleted from our records entirely, email quietcompass.store@gmail.com and we'll remove it within 48 hours.
